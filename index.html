The Ethical Implications of Artificial Intelligence

Introduction

The past few years have shown an increase in the use and development of artificial intelligence, and while they are still a ways off from what we see depicted in science fiction, they are inching closer as companies like Google and Facebook/Meta are developing their own respective artificial intelligences. As they and other companies further develop and improve upon artificial intelligence, we may soon find ourselves cooperating with another form of intelligent life, and with that, the ethical and social issues that we may face. Here, I will discuss some ethical and social norms that I believe we should consider if we are to include artificial intelligence in our future.
