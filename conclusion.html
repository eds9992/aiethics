The Ethical Implications of Artificial Intelligence

Conclusion

While we have discussed artificial intelligence and some ethics regarding the field, there is so much more to the conversation. Scientists and philosophers have debated the topic for decades, and we still have not reached a definite conclusion, and it is doubtful that we ever will. The purpose of this was not to reach a conclusion, but rather to help think about considerations should we interact with a theoretical super intelligent AI.
